🤖 AI Lead Intelligence
Auto-Scan — Runs the moment you import
📂
Step 1 — Upload CSV
Import any campaign. AI enrichment starts automatically in the background — no action needed.
🌐
Step 2 — Google Search (SerpAPI)
Searches each seller name + city across the web. Finds Shopee stores, TikTok shops, Facebook pages, and more.
🧠
Step 3 — Claude Haiku AI Analysis
AI extracts: product category, price range, followers, rating, product count, last active date, Lazada status. Writes a pre-visit brief for Gabay.
🎯
Step 4 — Score Saved (0–100)
Within same campaign priority, Gabay's queue auto-sorts by AI score. Highest-potential leads always surface first.
⚡ Campaign Dashboard — AI Sweep Button
For existing campaigns uploaded before this feature, go to Campaign Dashboard → click Run AI Sweep on any campaign. It queues all un-scanned leads immediately. Check back in a few minutes to see scores and briefs appear.
