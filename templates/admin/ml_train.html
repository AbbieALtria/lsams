How Maria Scores Your Leads — Step by Step
1 · Priority Tier
P0 leads score highest. P1 next, P2 lower, P3 lowest. Campaign priority directly affects your score.
2 · Pipeline Stage
Negotiation > Attempting > Assigned. Leads deeper in the funnel get a higher base score automatically.
3 · Visit Momentum
Recent visits with positive outcomes (interested, callback) boost the score. Stale leads lose points over time.
4 · Follow-up Compliance
Did the agent follow up on time? On-time follow-ups add points. Missed ones reduce the score.
5 · Contact & Category
Complete contact info and a recognized product category add bonus points to the final score.
