📘 Campaign Overlap Report — Complete Guide
For supervisors & team leaders · No technical knowledge needed
🧭 What is this page for?
This page helps you compare two campaigns side by side to find sellers that appear in both campaigns at the same time.

Why does this matter? When a new campaign is uploaded (like RRLD Wave 2), some sellers from the old campaign (Legacy) may already be in the new list. This tool finds those duplicate sellers so you can decide what to do — for example, keep the Gabay assignments the same, or mark the old campaign as finished.
📊 What do the 4 numbers mean?
🔵
Campaign A Total
The total number of sellers in the NEW campaign you just uploaded (e.g. RRLD Wave 2). This is the campaign you want to activate.
⚪
Campaign B Total
The total number of sellers in the OLD campaign (e.g. Legacy). This is the campaign you are migrating away from.
🟡 Overlap
Sellers in BOTH
Sellers who appear in both campaigns at the same time. These are the sellers that need attention — we need to decide who handles them and what their status should be.
🟢 Already Visited
Safe — already worked on
Of the overlapping sellers, how many were already visited by a Gabay in the old campaign. Their visit history is safe and will not be lost. The red number below shows how many still need a visit.
📋 What does the table show?
Seller
The store or seller's name that appears in both campaigns.
Phone
The phone number on file. A dash (—) means none was provided.
Status in B
The current status of this seller in the old campaign (Campaign B).
● live = active   ● registration = almost done   ● attempting = being worked on
Assigned Gabay
Which Gabay (field agent) is currently handling this seller in the old campaign.
Visits
How many times a Gabay has already visited this seller (total check-ins logged).
Visited?
✓ Visited = at least one visit was logged.
Not yet = no visit recorded yet.
🔘 What do the buttons do?
⇄ Sync Assignments & Status → Wave 2
What it does: For every seller that appears in both campaigns, this button copies the Gabay name and seller status from the old campaign (Legacy) into the new campaign (Wave 2). So the same Gabay keeps their sellers — you don't have to reassign everyone manually.

⚠️ Nothing is deleted. Visit history stays in the old campaign. This just makes sure the new campaign starts with the right assignments.

When to use: After comparing, if you want the new campaign to start with all the same Gabay assignments already in place.
🚩 Set Legacy → P99 · Set Wave 2 → P1 Active
What it does: This button does two things at the same time:
  1. Archives the old campaign (Legacy) by setting its priority to 99 — meaning it moves to the bottom of the list and is no longer active.
  2. Activates the new campaign (Wave 2) by setting it to Priority 1 — meaning it becomes the main active campaign your Gabays work from.

⚠️ Use this with care. This changes which campaign is "live". Do this after syncing assignments (button above), so your Gabays are already set up in Wave 2.

When to use: When you are ready to officially switch from the old campaign to the new one.
📥 Download CSV
What it does: Downloads the full list of overlapping sellers as a spreadsheet file (CSV — opens in Excel or Google Sheets). The file includes seller name, phone, status, assigned Gabay, visit count, and whether they were visited.

When to use: When you need to review the overlap list offline, share it with management, or print it for a team meeting.
