Good to Know
  • Column names are not case-sensitive — SELLER_NAME and seller_name both work
  • Duplicate lazada_id values are automatically skipped
  • Rows with blank seller names are skipped
  • You can import in multiple batches — duplicates won't be added twice
  • After import, all leads go to Pool status — unassigned
