How Lead Assignment Works
After importing leads, they sit in the Pool — unassigned to anyone. The Manager or Admin uses the Assignment Center to distribute leads to each Gabay. Gabay then see only their assigned leads when they log in.

VW vs VW-RRLD
VW Verified Seller — basic Lazada activation
VW-RRLD Verified + RRLD accreditation
Both are set by the Manager when approving a registration. A lead with either becomes Matched instead of just Live.

Good to Know
  • Column names are not case-sensitive — SELLER_NAME and seller_name both work
  • Duplicate lazada_id values are automatically skipped
  • Rows with blank seller names are skipped
  • You can import in multiple batches — duplicates won't be added twice
  • After import, all leads go to Pool status — unassigned
