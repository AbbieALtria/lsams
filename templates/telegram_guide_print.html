Log your visits from Telegram —

No need to open the app every time. Just send a message or voice note to the LSAMS Bot and your visit is automatically saved to the system.
2
easy steps
1
One-Time Setup
Do this once only
1
Open Telegram on your phone
You need to have the Telegram app installed.
2
Search for the LSAMS Bot
In the Telegram search bar, type:
lsams_notify_bot
3
Tap START
Open the bot and tap the blue START button at the bottom.
4
Send your LSAMS username
Type and send (replace with your actual username):
/start your_lsams_username
Example: if your LSAMS login is juan123, type:
/start juan123
✅
Bot replies: "Linked! Welcome!"
Setup is complete! You will never need to do this again.
⚠️ Don't know your username?
Ask your supervisor — it's the same username you use to log in to LSAMS.

Visited [seller name] — [outcome]

✅ What Happens When You Send
1. Bot finds the seller in your lead list
2. Visit is saved to LSAMS automatically
3. Your supervisor is notified right away
4. Lead status updates if needed
5. Bot replies with a confirmation message
