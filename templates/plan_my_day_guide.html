What is Plan My Day?

Plan My Day lets you build your own field route for the day — you pick the sellers, LSAMS builds the most efficient travel order. No more AI-suggested routes mixing sellers from different cities. You are in control of where you go.

Step-by-Step Guide
Step 1
Open the Feature
Go to Plan My Day
From the Gabay home screen, tap the 🗺️ Plan My Day button. You will see a selection screen showing all your active leads — these are the sellers currently assigned to you with status Assigned, Attempting, Negotiation, or Registration.
🗺️ Tap Plan My Day on Home Shows all your active leads Works offline for viewing
Tip: Plan My Day only includes leads assigned to you. If you don't see a seller you visited, ask your supervisor to assign them to your account.
Step 2
Filter by City
Use City Chips to Narrow Down Stops
At the top of the selection screen you will see colored chips for each city where your leads are located. Tap a city chip to show only leads from that city. Tap All Cities to see everything. This helps you quickly focus on one area for the day.
1Look at the city chip row at the top of the screen
2Tap the city you plan to work in today
3The list filters — only that city's sellers appear
4Tap All Cities to go back to the full list
Tap city chip → filters instantly All Cities → shows everything
Step 3
Choose Stops
Select the Sellers You Will Visit Today
Tap the checkbox on the left of each seller card to add them to your route. The button at the bottom shows how many stops you have selected so far. You can select up to 10 stops per route. Use Select All to pick all visible leads in the current city, or Clear to uncheck all.
1Tap a seller's checkbox to select them
2Selected card turns purple — you'll see the stop count update at the bottom
3Use Select All to pick all leads in the filtered city at once
4Use Clear to start over
5Max 10 stops — if you need more, plan a second route after the first
⚠️ No address = skipped. Sellers without a complete address cannot be added to a map route. If you see a red "No address" warning on a card, ask your supervisor to update that seller's address in the system.
Step 4
Build Route
Tap "Build My Route" to Calculate the Best Order
Once you have selected your stops, tap the Build My Route (X stops) button at the bottom of the screen. LSAMS will automatically arrange your selected stops in the shortest travel-time order using Google Maps. The result is your optimized route for the day.
Purple gradient button at bottom Shortest path calculated automatically Must select at least 1 stop
Best Practice: Select sellers from the same city for the most practical route. Mixing cities creates long travel times between stops.
Step 5
Navigate
Open in Waze or Google Maps
The route view shows your stops in optimized order with numbered circles (1, 2, 3…). At the top you will see two launch buttons — tap Open in Maps to open the full route in Google Maps, or Start in Waze to begin navigation from Stop 1 immediately. Each stop card also has individual Waze and Maps buttons if you want to navigate to a specific stop directly.
1Tap Open in Maps (blue) to open the full multi-stop route in Google Maps
2OR tap Start in Waze (light blue) to navigate from Stop 1 in Waze
3On each stop card, tap the individual Waze or Maps button to go to that specific seller
4After arriving, tap Check In on that stop card
🔵 Open in Maps → full route 🔵 Start in Waze → turn-by-turn Per-stop nav buttons too
Step 6
Check In
Log Your Visit at Each Stop
When you arrive at a seller's location, tap Check In on that stop card. This opens the full check-in form where you record the outcome, notes, and photos. After submitting, come back to the route view and continue to the next stop. Tap Plan a Different Route at the bottom when you are ready to build a new route.
1Arrive at the seller's location
2Tap Check In (green button) on the stop card
3Choose the outcome — Interested, Callback, Not Home, Rejected, Registered, or Follow Up
4Add your notes and upload photos if required
5Tap Submit — the visit is logged
6Return to the route view and proceed to the next stop

Tips & Best Practices
✅ Stick to one city per route. Selecting sellers from a single city gives you the shortest travel times and the most efficient day. Use the city filter chips to focus your selection.
✅ Max 10 stops. The route optimizer works best with 10 or fewer stops. If you have more leads to visit, complete your first route and build a second one.
✅ Missing address = skipped. Sellers shown with a red "No address" warning cannot be included in a map route. Tap "View Lead" on their card to see their details, then contact your supervisor to update the address.
✅ Check In from the route view. After navigating to each stop, come back to LSAMS and tap "Check In" on that stop's card — this ensures the system records the correct seller for your visit.
✅ Radar section. Scroll down past your stop list to see the Seller Radar — unassigned sellers in your city that no one is visiting yet. These are bonus leads you can check in on if you have time.
Troubleshooting
Problem Solution
I see "No active leads" Your supervisor hasn't assigned any leads to you yet, or all your leads are in a closed/pool status. Contact your supervisor.
Seller shows "No address" warning The address field is empty for that lead. Ask your supervisor to add the complete address. You can still View Lead and Check In manually from the list.
"Build My Route" button not working Make sure you have selected at least one stop. The button is disabled when 0 stops are selected.
Route says "Your selected order" instead of "AI-optimized" The Google Maps API key may not be configured. The system still shows your selected stops — navigate to each one manually using the Waze/Maps buttons.
I selected 12 sellers but only see 10 stops The route optimizer supports a maximum of 10 stops. The extra 2 are shown in a banner — plan a second route after completing the first 10.
