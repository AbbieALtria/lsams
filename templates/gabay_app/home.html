🎙 Voice Check-In Guide
Updated Steps — June 2026
Here are the 7 easy steps for Voice Check-In. Read all before you start! 👇
1
🎙 Tap the mic button
On the Check-In page, tap the big mic 🎙. It turns red and starts a timer — you are now recording.
2
🗣 Speak clearly about your visit
Say the seller name, what happened (interested / rejected / registered), any follow-up, and notes. You can speak in Tagalog, English, Bisaya, or mixed.
3
⏹ Tap Stop when done speaking
A blue box appears showing how long you recorded. You can play it back before sending.
4
▶ Listen back to your recording
Tap the Play button to hear yourself. If something is wrong or unclear, tap 🔄 Re-record to record again for free.
5
🤖 Tap "Send to Maria"
Maria will listen to your recording and type out what she heard. This needs internet and takes about 2–5 seconds.
6
🧠 Read what Maria heard — then confirm
NEW
Maria types out the text on screen. Wait for the button to turn blue and say "✅ Yes, that's correct" — then tap it to confirm.
💡 Tip — Button still gray?
If the button still shows "⏳ Reading…" and you already read the text — just tap the text itself to skip the animation. The button will turn blue right away so you can confirm.
7
✅ Tap "Apply to Form" — then Submit
All fields are filled automatically! Review the details, then tap Submit Check-In. You'll see a green confirmation when done.
