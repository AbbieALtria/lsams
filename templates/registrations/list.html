How Registrations Work
1️⃣
Lead Visits
Gabay agent visits the seller and logs a visit with outcome Interested or Registered
2️⃣
Registration Created
A registration record is automatically created when a lead reaches Registration status
3️⃣
Documents Uploaded
Upload seller's 5 required Lazada documents (DTI, ID, bank, etc.) in the detail view
4️⃣
Lazada Approves
Submit to Lazada → status changes to Approved → seller goes Live
Draft → created, not submitted Pending → submitted, waiting Approved → Lazada accepted Rejected → needs correction
