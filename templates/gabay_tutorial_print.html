Scene 1
Introduction
Welcome to Gabay
Gabay is your field companion. It tells you exactly where to go, who to visit, and how to log every interaction — all from your phone. Your supervisor can see your progress in real time, so keep the app updated as you work through the day.
Scene 2
Your Queue
Your Lead Queue — Campaign Priority
Your home screen shows the lead queue. Leads from the highest-priority campaign appear first — marked with a gold P1 badge. Blue P2/P3 badges are next. Always work from top to bottom. Your supervisor controls which campaign is most urgent and the queue updates automatically — you don't need to do anything special.
P1 gold badge = highest priority P2/P3 blue = next Always work top to bottom
Tip: If you see a P1 badge, that campaign's leads must be visited first — your supervisor has marked it urgent.
Scene 3
Navigation
Getting to the Seller's Location
Tap any lead card to see the seller's full details — name, address, phone, and Lazada ID. Tap the Map button to open navigation directly in Google Maps. The system also shows you sellers who are physically close to each other so you can group your visits efficiently and save travel time.
📍 Map button → Google Maps Group nearby sellers Full seller details on card
Scene 4
Radar
Radar — Sellers Near You Right Now
The Radar screen shows all sellers within a few kilometers of your current GPS location. Unvisited sellers appear as orange dots. If you finish your assigned leads early, use Radar to find nearby unassigned sellers you can visit as bonus calls. Tap any dot to see details and navigate there.
🟠 Orange dot = unvisited Use for bonus visits Based on your live GPS
Scene 5
Check In
Logging a Visit — Step by Step
When you arrive at the seller's location, tap Check In on the lead card. The system captures your GPS location automatically — you must be within range of the address. Then choose the outcome of the visit.
1Tap the lead card to open seller details
2Tap Check In — GPS is captured
3Choose the outcome from the list
4Add your notes in the text box
5Upload photos (if required)
6Tap Submit
Scene 6
Outcomes
Choosing the Right Outcome
Always choose the most accurate outcome. This data goes directly to your supervisor and to Lazada. Use the correct code — wrong outcomes affect your conversion rate score.
✓Interested — seller wants to register, follow-up scheduled
✓Already Registered — seller is already live on Lazada
✓Not Interested — seller declined, explain why in notes
✓No Contact — store closed or number not responding
✓Follow Up — needs another visit, pick a date
✓Invalid Address — address does not exist or is wrong
Scene 7
Photos
Uploading Visit Photos
For Interested and Follow-Up outcomes, photos are required. Take a photo of the seller's storefront and any products they sell. Tap the camera icon to open your phone camera, take the photo, and it uploads instantly. Photos prove the visit happened and help your supervisor verify quality. Missing photos will be flagged in the Daily Digest.
Required for Interested / Follow Up Storefront + products Missing = flagged by supervisor
Scene 8
WhatsApp Bot
WhatsApp Bot — Automatic Notifications
When you log in to Gabay, your supervisor receives a WhatsApp message automatically saying you are online. When you complete a visit and mark a seller as Interested, another alert is sent. You do not need to send any messages yourself — the bot handles it. Make sure your WhatsApp number matches what is registered in your user account, otherwise alerts will not reach your supervisor.
Auto-login alert Auto-visit alert on Interested No action needed from you Number must match your account
Scene 9
Reports
Your Personal Performance Report
The Reports tab shows your personal performance for the current month: total visits logged, sellers marked Interested, registrations confirmed, and your conversion rate. Your supervisor can also see this. Tap any day in the calendar to review what you submitted that day. Keep your daily visit count above your target — shown at the top of the screen.
Monthly summary Daily calendar view Target shown at top Supervisor can see this too
Scene 10
Registration
Submitting a Registration
When a seller agrees to register on Lazada, open the registration form from the lead card. Fill in their full name, shop name, national ID number, and email. Take a photo of their ID document. Submit the form — it goes directly to the Lazada onboarding team. The lead status updates to Registration Submitted automatically.
1Open lead → tap Submit Registration
2Fill seller details + shop name
3Photo of National ID
4Submit → status updates automatically
Scene 11
Daily Tips
Best Practices for Field Work
Follow these habits every day to keep your numbers strong and your supervisor happy.
☀️Log in to Gabay first thing every morning — triggers your supervisor's online alert
🗺️Check your queue before leaving — plan your route for the day
📍Check In the moment you arrive — don't do it before or after the visit
📷Always take photos — even for No Contact (photo of closed shop)
📝Add real notes — "spoke to owner, interested in electronics category" is good; "visited" is not
🕐Log visits immediately — don't batch them at the end of the day
🏆Hit your daily target before 5 PM
Scene 12
Summary
You're Ready — Go!
You now know everything you need to be a great Gabay field agent. Start at the top of your queue, work the P1 leads first, log every visit honestly, upload your photos, and keep your notes detailed. Your supervisor is watching your real-time progress and is there to help. Good luck!
P1 campaign leads first Log every visit Real notes + photos Supervisor sees you live
