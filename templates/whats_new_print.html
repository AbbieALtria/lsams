📊 Seller Intelligence
ENHANCED — Leads Page
💚 Seller Health Grade (A–D)
Every lead now has a Health Grade replacing the old Priority label.
A 82 B 61 C 34 D 12
Grade A = 75–100 pts · B = 50–74 · C = 25–49 · D = 0–24
Lead Detail shows grade, score/100, days since last visit, total visit count, and recent outcome trend.
NEW — Visit Form
🏪 Mall / LazMall Flag
Gabay agents can now flag a seller as "inside a mall" when recording a visit. Provides a mall name (SM North, Ayala, LazMall, etc.).
  • Managers can filter: All / Outside Mall / 🏪 In Mall
  • Purple 🏪 Mall badge appears on lead list
  • Scout auto-detects mall keywords and pre-checks the flag
  • Another Lazada team manages mall sellers — flag helps coordination
NEW — Visit Form
🏷 Competitor Brand
Log which competitor brand or platform the seller currently carries. Shows on Lead Detail (editable) and helps managers see market share patterns.
e.g. Shopee Mall
NEW — Lead Detail
🏷 Seller Tags
Add custom labels to any lead. Click a preset tag or type your own. Visible in the lead list as blue pills.
Hot Lead Key Account Declined
AUTO — Each Visit
📅 Next Visit Date
Automatically calculated from last visit outcome. Shows in lead list under Last Visit.
  • Interested → +3 days
  • Follow-up → +5 days
  • No Contact → +2 days
  • Not Interested → +14 days
