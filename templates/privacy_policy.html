Altria Management Corporation · LSAMS

Effective date: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Altria Management Corporation ("Altria", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the LSAMS (Lazada Seller Acquisition Management System) platform, including its WhatsApp Business integration ("LSAMS Communication Hub").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information through the LSAMS platform and its WhatsApp integration:

  • Agent information: Name, mobile phone number, login activity, GPS location during field visits
  • Seller/Lead information: Business name, contact details, visit notes, registration status
  • WhatsApp messages: Text messages and voice notes sent by registered field agents to the LSAMS bot number for the purpose of logging visit reports
  • Usage data: Login timestamps, page visits within the LSAMS web application

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To record and track seller visit outcomes by field agents
  • To notify supervisors and managers of field activity in real time
  • To generate reports and performance metrics for Lazada campaign management
  • To operate the WhatsApp bot that allows agents to log visits via chat

3. WhatsApp Integration

The LSAMS Communication Hub uses the WhatsApp Business API (Meta Platforms, Inc.) to send and receive messages. Only registered and verified field agents whose phone numbers are enrolled in the LSAMS system can interact with the bot. Messages sent to the bot are stored in the LSAMS database solely for operational and reporting purposes.

We do not sell, share, or use WhatsApp message data for advertising or any purpose outside of LSAMS field operations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Lazada Philippines — as part of the seller acquisition program we operate on their behalf
  • Railway (hosting provider) — the LSAMS platform is hosted on Railway's cloud infrastructure
  • Meta Platforms — solely for the purpose of delivering WhatsApp messages via their Business API

5. Data Retention

Visit logs, lead data, and agent records are retained for the duration of the Altria–Lazada engagement and for a minimum of 1 year after termination for audit purposes. WhatsApp message content is retained as part of the visit record.

6. Data Security

All data is stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. Access is restricted to authorized Altria supervisors, administrators, and assigned field agents. We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

7. Your Rights

Registered LSAMS users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us at the email below. Requests will be processed within 30 days.

8. User Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data from the LSAMS system, contact us at ibrahimcsc@icloud.com with your name and registered phone number. We will delete your account and associated data within 7 business days.
