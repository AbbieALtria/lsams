How This Forecast Is Calculated
📐 Formula
conv_rate = live ÷ total_assigned
reg_prob = conv_rate × 3.0 (max 35%)
nego_prob = conv_rate × 1.0 (max 10%)
att_prob = conv_rate × 0.3 (max 3%)

All × working_days_factor

forecast = (Reg × reg_prob)
          + (Nego × nego_prob)
          + (Attempt × att_prob)

projected_live = live_now + forecast
